Arlington Threading


Love yourself, Arlington Threading is here to focus and shape your eyebrows.


Arlington Threading is Located at 2420 Wilson Blvd. Suite 200 Arlington VA 22201. Our team specializes in the art of threading, which is a natural & unique way of shaping eyebrows and hair removal using a regular cotton sewing thread. We are here to provide a service with a personal touch to both men and women of all ages.
Persons who thread once at Arlington Threading will become a fan for a lifetime. Come and experience the service of our qualified professionals. Please, visit us at 2420 Wilson Blvd. Suite 200 Arlington VA 22201 to materialize your dreams of perfect eyebrow shaping.
